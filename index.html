UNC Biology Department Ceremony

Full Description (Word Document)

310 South Rd, Chapel Hill, NC 27514

The biology department ceremony will be on Saturday, May 7th from 6pm to 7:30pm. The doors will open at 5:30pm. Click the link below for the full description. Please bring your own bottled water. There are water fountains inside Carmichael. There will be PLENTY of seating available.

This one is the major-specific graduation. If you want to come that would be great, but the one that I hope you can come to is the UNC Spring Commencement. Seating is alphabetical, so I will most likely be near the back of the class.

This is a formal event. However, there is no dress code for guests.

The reception is from 7:30pm to 8:15pm outdoors next to the ceremony at Hooker Field. Light refreshments and drinks will be covered by tents.

Parking: The Bell Tower Parking Deck is reserved for biology ceremony guests! There will be someome there to monitor. It takes about 15 minutes (3.5 blocks) to walk from there to the ceremony. There will be two golf carts to take guests to the ceremony if walking is an issue.

UNC Spring Commencement Ceremony

Official Website - UNC Spring Commencement

104 Stadium Dr, Chapel Hill, NC 27514

The UNC Commencement Ceremony will be on Sunday, May 8th from 9am to approximately 11:30am. Click the link below to view the official website! Scroll down until you see "for families".

If you cannot ascend or descend stairs in the stadium or the shuttle, please send an email to access@unc.edu to explore seating options. Please arrive early to identify a seat near the concourse level that best fits your needs. The seating is available on a first come, first serve basis.

The cost to attend is free and there is no registration required. Try to sit in the same vicinity as the other people!

The ceremony will be livestreamed and captioned, making it available for individuals unable to make it! The link will be available the day of commencement thorugh the link at the top of the page.

Afterparty

100 Rock Haven Rd, Carrboro, NC 27510

The graduation party will be on Sunday, May 8th from 12pm to approximately 5pm. There will be a pool, a pool table, ping-pong, and other games and such! Feel free to bring anything you'd like. There is a locker room and shower area to change in after the pool.

Food: Catered by Cosmic Cantina, one of Franklin Street's best authentic, Mexican food restaurants. It will be a create-your-own burrito bar with steak, chicken, vegan chorizo as the meats. Side items will be rice, black beans, pinto beans, lettuce, sour cream, hot sauce, pico de gallo, guacamole, queso dip, chips, and flour and corn tortillas.

Contact me if you have any dietary restrictions such as gluten free.
